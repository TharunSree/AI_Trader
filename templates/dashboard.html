Rejection Guide

Concepts Explanation:

Strategy Underperformance

This means the variant's virtual trading returns (PnL) were lower than the parent model over the test window.

E.g., parent gained +5.2% but variant only gained +2.1% (or lost capital).

Directives Guide:
  • Tighten stop-loss: Restricts downside risk by exiting trades quicker when price drops.
  • Improve timing: Adjusts indicator trigger bounds to avoid early/late entries.
  • Optimize win rate: AI focuses on high-conviction trades, ignoring noisy setups.
  • Reduce frequency: Trade less often; only enter during clear momentum runs.
