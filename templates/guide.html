Feature Sets & Strategies

When training a model, you must select a Feature Set. This defines the mathematical indicators the neural network will analyze to make predictions. Here is a breakdown of the available intelligence protocols:

Alpha Discovery

Indicators: SMA (20, 50, 200), RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, ATR.

A balanced, omni-directional strategy. Looks for broad market inefficiencies and standard technical alignments. Great baseline for all environments.

Volatility Breakout

Indicators: Bollinger Bands, ATR, ADX, Momentum.

Aggressively targets explosive price movements. Best used when anticipating sudden market shifts or news-driven events that shatter standard resistance levels.

Intraday Reversal

Indicators: RSI, Stochastic, Williams %R, CCI.

Contrarian protocol that seeks overbought/oversold exhaustion points to bet against the immediate trend. Thrives in choppy, non-trending markets.

Macro Trend

Indicators: SMA (50, 200), EMA (21, 55), Ichimoku Cloud.

Patience-heavy trend-following system. Ignores short-term noise and only executes when long-term moving averages align. Excellent for holding massive winners.

Institutional Value

Indicators: VWAP, OBV, MFI, Volume.

Follows the money. Analyzes volume footprints to detect large institutional accumulation or distribution. If whales are buying, this model buys.

Growth Momentum

Indicators: ROC, MACD Histogram, ADX, PSAR.

High-octane trend rider. Buys assets that are already moving fast and attempts to surf the momentum before it slows down. Cuts losers quickly.

Autonomous Execution & Strict Bounding

When a compiled model is launched into the Runner Fleet, it subscribes directly to live market ticks. The engine uses strict mathematical overrides to protect you:

  • Partition Limits: A model given a $1,000 budget will never exceed $1,000 in notional exposure. Execution dynamically queries the TradeLog array to summarize valid liquidity margins.
  • Dynamic Hard-Loss Caps (12%): Instead of relying on manual <input> limits, the backend autonomously establishes a maximum 12% portfolio downside barrier based on initial equity upon boot. If the algorithmic cluster plummets past this 12% peak draw threshold, all sockets are permanently severed to protect the primary capital.
  • 1-Cent Defensive Mechanics: The execution wrapper algorithmically forces an absolute sell condition if the global Alpaca state crosses exactly $0.01 threshold per share to immediately secure trailing profits dynamically.
